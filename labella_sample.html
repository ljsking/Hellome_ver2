PRINT / 2008 /
LA BELLA FIGURA MILANESE
[Milan's Beautiful Figure]
Cultural Research Report
297x210mm
Concept, Photography, Layout Design: In-ah Shin
While living in Milan as a part of my exchange study in 2008, I observed and interviewed eldery and their fashion. Though it started off with personal interest, the study revealed some of the compelling aspects of the Milanese soceity today: the fashion capital confronting aging society.

This study was presented in street fashion photography format, often found in fashion magazines.
